How to Play

Goal: Reach the finish line before others while dodging obstacles. If you crash, you restart at the beginning.

Controls (4 Rails):

◀ ▶ or [A / D]: Switch between the 4 fixed rail tracks.

⬆ or [W / Space]: Jump to dodge low obstacles.

+ / - or [E / Q]: Accelerate or decelerate your mine cart.

Power-Ups (Rainbow Pellets 🌈):

Collide with a rainbow pellet to gain a Pac-Ability. Activate it with the yellow [USE] button or [S].

Pac-Dots & Dash:

Collect yellow dots scattered on the rails. Collect 10 dots to charge up your QUICK DASH! Fire with [Shift] or the blue [DASH] button.
